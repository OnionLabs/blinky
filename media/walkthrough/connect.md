# Connect Your Board

1. **Plug in** your ESP32 board via USB
2. Run **blinky: Connect to Board** from the Command Palette (`Ctrl+Shift+P`)
3. If multiple boards are detected, pick the right port from the list

The extension auto-detects common ESP32 USB bridges (CP210x, CH340, FTDI) and shows them at the top of the port picker.

> **Tip:** Enable `blinky.autoConnect` in Settings to reconnect automatically on startup.
